Our Story

Building a Legacy of Excellence

Stumut Engineering Limited was founded with a singular vision: to raise the standard of construction and engineering across Nigeria. From our first project, we committed to engineering precision, transparent communication, and client-first delivery.

Today, we operate as a nationally trusted general contractor across residential, commercial, and civil infrastructure sectors — managing everything from bespoke homes to multi-storey commercial developments and large-scale civil works.
